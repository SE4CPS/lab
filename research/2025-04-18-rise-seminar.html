"Can we generate UML architecture diagram from current installed updates?"

— Architect question, 2025

Why LLM/RAG instead of Logical Languages (SQL/PL)?

  • Natural Language Friendly: Ask questions in plain English
  • Contextual Reasoning: Connects scattered data across docs
  • Unstructured Data Access: Works with APIs, text, CSVs—not just databases
  • Faster Iteration: Skip schema design and joins—query semantically
  • Scales with Knowledge: Retrieval + reasoning adapts as data grows
